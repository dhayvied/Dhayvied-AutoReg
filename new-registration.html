In Lagos and Nigeria as a whole there are several categories in which you can register your vehicle under; But here in Dhavied AutoReg, We only deal on the four major types of Registration which are:

  • Private Plates: This is the most common types of number plates in Nigeria. You can see them around whenever you hit the road. Background – White, Lettering – Blue.
  • Commercial Plate: This type of number plates can be seen in commercial buses. Background – White, Lettering – Red.
  • Government Plate: Background – White, Lettering – Green.
  • Special/Fancy Plate: Background – White, Lettering – Blue.
