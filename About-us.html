Dhayvied AutoReg

Dhayvied AutoReg in conjunction with Lagos State Government and Lagos Computerised Vehicle Inspection Service (LACVIS) was established in 2018 to help Lagosians ease the stress of getting your vehicle Registered.We are leading the way as Agents for AutoReg and Renewal in Lagos State.

OUR VISION
To be the most preferred Agent for vehicle Registration in Lagos State.

OUR MISSION
To collaborate with the Lagos State Government in issuing Genuine Documents to Vehicle owners.
To Partner with relevant agencies, stakeholders and the general public.
